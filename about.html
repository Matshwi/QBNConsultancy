FOUNDER | SELF-MASTERY COACH

Queen has devoted her life to understand how emotions and thoughts impact mental, physical and emotional health. She uses the SELF-Model to help her clients to discover their abilities to take control of their own lives.

She has been a life coach for more than 20 years, and she identiifes solutions that help her willing clients to overcome their daily personal challenges.

Queen is also an author and a pastor; her calling comes with the gift of tapping into spiritual principles that respond to practical life challenges. She practicals what she teaches and has a proven track record to transforming the lives of others.

She has been invited to many prestigious platforms as a motivational speaker and address any topic that is given to her.
